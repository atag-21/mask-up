ABOUT

Mask Up is a demonstrative web app that simulates a transit
system's (say Delhi Metro) entry point, only allowing people who
not only have enough debit in their transit card, but are also
wearing a mask,to enter the transit system. It leverages
computer vision and machine learning to detect if a person
is wearing a face mask while checking against a minimum fair
to decide whether the transit card is valid for a journey
